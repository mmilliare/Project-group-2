Welcome to the website about Our Project: Algorithm Design Group 2!

The general idea for this assignment is that we work together in a group to curate a dataset, and design an algorithm to process that dataset in a meaningful way. We are publishing both our dataset and our algorithm design on GitHub, alongside a the present website with some basic information on the project, dataset and algorithm, that is be hosted with GitHub Pages.
The assigment contains 5 steps, namely:
→ Step 1 of the Assignment: Conceptualisation;
→ Step 2 of the Assignment: Curate Our Dataset;
→ Step 3 of the Assignment: Design Our Algorithm;
→ Step 4 of the Assignment: Design a Small Website for Our Project;
→ Step 5 of the Assignment: Publish Our Dataset, Algorithm, and Website on GitHub.

Context

This website is part of a project from the Datalogical Thinking course at the Borås University (Sweden). For more information, please refer to the template's GitHub repository.
